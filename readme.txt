== Description ==

This plugin allows you to manage and display upcoming events organized by Oldskoolbelgium. It provides several shortcodes that can be used to display event listings and details on your website.

Shortcodes provided by this plugin:

[tiqs-events]: Displays a list of all events.
[tiqs-events-upcoming]: Displays a list of upcoming events.
[tiqs-event-details]: Displays detailed information about a specific event.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I purchase tickets for an event? =
You can purchase tickets directly through our website. Simply navigate to the event page and click on the "Tickets" button. From there, you will be prompted to select your desired ticket type and quantity, and proceed to checkout.

= Can I get a refund if I can no longer attend an event? =
Refund policies vary depending on the event organizer. Please refer to the event page or contact the organizer directly for more information.

= Are events canceled in case of bad weather? =
This depends on the type of event and the severity of the weather conditions. In some cases, events may be postponed or canceled, while in others they may proceed as planned. Please check the event page or contact the organizer for more information.

= Is there an age restriction for events? =
Age restrictions vary depending on the event. Some events may be all ages, while others may have specific age restrictions or require a valid ID for entry. Please refer to the event page or contact the organizer for more information.

= Are pets allowed at events? =
Pets are generally not allowed at events unless they are service animals. However, policies may vary depending on the event and venue. Please refer to the event page or contact the organizer for more information.

= Can I bring food or drinks to an event? =
Outside food and drinks are typically not allowed at events unless otherwise specified. However, policies may vary depending on the event and venue. Please refer to the event page or contact the organizer for more information.

= How can I become a sponsor or vendor for an event? =
Please contact the event organizer directly to inquire about sponsor or vendor opportunities. Contact information can usually be found on the event page or website.
